About

This website is maintained by Ali Soroudi as a personal teaching and resource hub for optimization courses, with emphasis on power systems, energy systems and practical computational modelling.

The aim is to make optimization easier to learn through clear formulations, reproducible examples, and application-driven case studies. Course material can be expanded here over time with lecture pages, datasets, code repositories and project briefs.
